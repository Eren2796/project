FAQ

Overview

What is ArtHub? ArtHub is a social platform and online gallery dedicated to artists, creators, and art enthusiasts. It allows users to discover, share, and promote various forms of artwork, including paintings, drawings, sculptures, digital art, photography, and more. How do I create an ArtHub account? To create an ArtHub account, visit the ArtHub website or download the ArtHub app from your device's app store. Follow the registration process by providing your email address, creating a username, and setting a password. Once registered, you can start exploring and interacting with the ArtHub community.

How does it work?

How do I upload my artwork to ArtHub? Uploading your artwork to ArtHub is simple. Log in to your ArtHub account and navigate to the "Upload" section. Choose the type of artwork you want to upload (e.g., painting, sculpture, photography), fill in the required information (title, description, category, tags), and upload the image or file of your artwork. You can also add multiple images to showcase your work from different angles.
